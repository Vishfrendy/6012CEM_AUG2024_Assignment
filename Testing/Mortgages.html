FAQ

What is a mortgage?

A mortgage is a loan specifically for purchasing property, where the property itself serves as collateral.

How do I know how much I can borrow?

Your borrowing capacity is determined by your income, credit history, and overall financial commitments.

What are the fees associated with mortgages?

Common fees include application fees, valuation fees, and legal fees, but many lenders offer fee waivers.

How long does it take to get approved?

Approval times can vary but typically range from a few days to several weeks, depending on the lender and complexity of your application.
