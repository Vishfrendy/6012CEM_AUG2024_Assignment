Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact support?

Our support team is available 24/7. You can reach out via email, phone, or the contact form on our website.

Are all listings verified?

Yes, we thoroughly verify all listings to ensure they meet our standards of accuracy and reliability.

How do I schedule a property viewing?

Simply click on the listing and select "Schedule Viewing." You can choose a date and time that works best for you.

Is there a fee for using HomeBound?

No, browsing and contacting property listings is free. We charge a small fee for premium features, if you choose to use them.

Can I list my property on HomeBound?

Absolutely! Just register as a seller or agent and follow the steps in our listing guide to publish your property.
